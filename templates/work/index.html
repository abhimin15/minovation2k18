About us

Department of Mining Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology (BHU) is the oldest mining institute in India. IIT(BHU) is celebrating 100 years of engineering education this year. MINOVATION 2018, the annual Techno-Management Fest of the Mining Society, Department of Mining Engineering, will be held from 28th-30th september, 2018. MINOVATION (formerly METTLE) is seen as the largest event of its kind in India with remarkable participation from across the country.
Alumni from prominent mining, mine machinery and allied companies are expected to grace this occasion. Plethora of technical events, competitions, workshops, guest lectures from eminent personalities and Alumni Meet will be organised. This will provide an unparalled platform for the future technocrats and entrepreneurs to tap into their hidden potential..

PAPER PRESENTATION

In today's dynamic world the need of the hour is to have a sustainable, economic and efficient plan to accompalish any task. MINOVATION 2018 invites students/scholars to present their papers and contribute to fostering the Industry-Academia Collaboration.

Eligibility

The participant must be pursuing an UG/PG/Diploma program in Mining or allied (Petroleum, Geology) from a recognized institute and if you are from any other engineering branch, then you must have done or should have been doing any project which is directly related to Mining engineering. The participant may be asked to prove his/her eligibility.

Event Structures

The event consists of two rounds. First is online submission and the second is to be conducted at IIT (BHU) Varanasi during MINOVATION'18. First round is Online submission of abstract in pdf format to be emailed to events.minovation@gmail.com and based on which teams will be short listed for the next round. Second round is a presentation cum questionnaire round where participants need to present their work before Judges and other teams.

Rules

  1. Individuals must register online to participate.
  2. Each team can contain a maximum of 4 participants
  3. For the preliminary round each team needs to prepare an abstract of their innovative idea.
  4. The abstract must contain the team name and the names of each member, and it should be mailed to events.minovation@gmail.com
  5. Project or paper must be related to Mining.
  6. Abstract shall not contain more than 200 words.
  7. Deadline for submission of abstract is 25.09.2018. Abstract will not be accepted after the deadline.
  8. Teams will be short listed on the basis of the merit of their submission. So try to cover all the important points of your work in the abstract.
  9. Short listed participants will be eligible to present their idea at IIT (BHU) Varanasi during MINOVATION'18.
  10. Teams short listed for the second round must come with a proper presentation along with at least 2 hardcopies of their presentation.
  11. Each team will be provided a time limit of 10 minutes to present their ideas.
  12. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
  13. Top three participants will get a certificate of merit and others will get a certificate of participation.

GEOCARTER

Geology is the core discipline of the earth sciences and encompasses many different phenomena, including plate tectonics and mountain building, volcanoes and earthquakes, and the long-term evolution of Earth’s atmosphere, surface and life. Because of the ever-increasing demand for resources, the growing exposure to natural hazards, and the changing climate, geology is of considerable societal relevance. This event will check your grip on geology with your engineering skills. The event contains interesting geological engineering problems with geological mapping and identification of different rock and minerals.

Eligibility

The participant must be pursuing an UG/PG/Diploma program in Mining or allied (Petroleum, Geology) from a recognized institute and if you are from any other engineering branch, then you must have done or should have been doing any project which is directly related to Mining engineering. The participant may be asked to prove his/her eligibility.

Event Structures

  • The event contains two rounds and both rounds will be held During Minovation’18 at Department of Mining Engineering IIT(BHU) Varanasi.
  • In the first round participants have to identify different minerals and rock on the basis of their characterstics and Second round will be subjective questioning round with some good geological problems and mapping. Participants are required to come with one geometry box per team for second round.
  • Both rounds contain some wettage for estimation of results, so participants have to attend both rounds.

Rules

  1. Only teams who have registered online on Minovation’18 would be able to participate.
  2. Each team can contain a maximum of 4 participants
  3. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
  4. Shoes are mandatory for each round.
